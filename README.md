# 📘 Forex Market Analysis – Complete Study Program

[![License: CC BY 4.0](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-CC_BY_4.0-lightgrey.svg)](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/)

A comprehensive, self-study program covering Forex, commodities, price action, ICT concepts, fundamental analysis, trade planning, risk management, and trading psychology.

This book is provided for free as an Open Educational Resource (OER).

---

##  About This Project ✍️

This is a complete 7-module course designed to take you from a complete beginner to an advanced trader, with a deep focus on institutional methodologies (ICT) and a strong emphasis on the psychology and risk management often missing from other resources.

###  What You'll Find Inside
*   **Forex Foundations:** Market structure, sessions, currency pairs, and commodities.
*   **Price Action & Candlesticks:** From single-candle patterns to complex structures.
*   **Chart Patterns:** Reversal and continuation patterns with measurement techniques.
*   **ICT Concepts (Deep Dive):** Order Blocks, FVGs, Liquidity, SMT Divergence, and the full ICT framework.
*   **Fundamental Analysis:** Central banks, COT report, economic calendars, and the Dollar Smile theory.
*   **Trade Planning:** A full top-down workflow, pre-trade checklists, and a trade journaling system.
*   **Risk & Psychology:** The most important module, covering drawdown management, the HALT rule, cognitive biases, and 4 in-depth case studies.

##  How to Use This Material

1.  Read the modules in order; each one builds on the last.
2.  Use the pre-trade checklists and the trade journal template to develop a disciplined process.
3.  Pay special attention to **Module 7** – it's the key to long-term success.
